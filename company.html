MERCURIO DESIGN LAB S.r.l. (MDL) is an award-winning Italian design studio that creates avant-garde concepts inspired by the vibrancy of Asia. Our team is composed of passionate architects, engineers, designers, and artists who integrate art with technology in the design process while respecting the three fundamental criteria: the functional, the aesthetic, and the social.

Since its inception, MDL has been a place for creative ideas and thoughts, with a high energy to generate architecturally meaningful work. With over a decade of growth, we have made many outstanding designs across different sectors- hospitality, mixed-use, commercial, sports and recreation, residential, and masterplan. Our projects are considered as artistic masterpieces, often set as a landmark of the location, thus, we make a special contribution to the architectural scene of the city through our creation of dramatic and futuristic buildings. Most of our undertakings are with repeated clients, often MNC’s, reaffirming our commitment, reliability, and trustworthiness.

A spin-off of the AMA Group, one of the largest architectural consortia in continental Europe for more than 50 years now, MDL masterfully manages to express its design potential. With a root still firmly planted in Italy, we have become a global company with particular expertise in the Asian market. Through our company’s Italian spirit and rich cultural heritage, our studio creates new milestones and commits with great passion to bring ideas and solutions into the fields of Architecture, Interior Design, and Industrial Design.

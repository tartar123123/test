Job Function:

Create landscapes, plan, and design the physical aspects of outdoor space of all MDL projects based on clients and standards.


Job Qualifications:
  • Candidate must possess at least Bachelor's Degree, Professional License (Passed Board/Bar/Professional License Exam) in Architecture or equivalent.
  • At least 5 years of working experience in the related field is required for this position.
  • Must be knowledgeable in using designing software: ArchiCAD, AutoCAD, Sketch-Up, and other related tools.
  • Produce detailed workings, drawings, and specifications
  • Specify the nature and quality of materials required.
  • Using creativity and the beauty of nature to create a harmonious, aesthetically pleasing, and functional area.
  • Researching topics such as plants and their ability to survive in an area, guidelines, and codes.
  • Design landscapes that satisfy their clients' needs while conforming to the laws and regulations of the areas in which the designs will be built.

Job Description:
Major duties and responsibilities
  1. Design and Presentation
    • Create plans and drawings using computer-aided design programs
    • Meet with clients, engineers, and building architects to understand the requirements of a project
    • Prepare site plans, specifications, and estimates
    • Select materials based on the client's desire and budget
  2. Research and Analyzation
    • Research and analyze environmental reports on land condition
    • Study climate parameters of the location of the projects
  3. Coordination
    • Discuss requirements with clients
    • Present proposals to the client
    • Overseeing projects as they progress

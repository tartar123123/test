Employment Type Independent Contractor

Job Functions:

Research, write, copywriting, copyedit, review, edit, proofread, and update all internal and external corporate communications.


Job Qualifications:
  • Background in communications, journalism, writing for print and/or broadcast
  • Experience in technical writing in all forms of media and communication
  • Experience in technical writing preferably in an architectural design company
  • Skilled in preparing comprehensive technical communications
  • Highly affluent in English (oral and written)

Job Description:

Major duties and responsibilities:
  1. Internal and External Communications
    • Communicate with colleagues and key spokespeople to gather information
    • Liaise with, and answer inquiries from, media individuals and other organizations

  2. Media Relations
    • Monitor publicity and collates published press releases
    • Research, write and distribute press releases to targeted media
  3. Content Writing
    • Maintain and update information on the organization’s website

  4. Reporting
    • Review and summarize feedback from press and social media sites
  5. General Marketing
    • Assist in planning, developing, and implementing PR strategies
    • Assist in collating and analyzing media coverage
    • Perform and/or assist in adhoc and other marketing activities such as organizing events like press conferences, exhibitions, etc.

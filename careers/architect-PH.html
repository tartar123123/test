Architect

Archi Team PH · Mandaluyong, NCR

Department Archi Team PH
Employment Type Independent Contractor
Minimum Experience Experienced

Job Function:

Assist the Design Architect/ Senior Design Architect in preparing design presentations and drawings in all the Design phases/aspects.

Job Qualifications:
  • BS Architecture Graduate
  • Licensed Architect
  • 6-10 years experience in Architecture Design for Hospitality

Job Description:
Major duties and responsibilities
  1. Design Solution
    • Assists the Design Architect in providing the design solution and strategy for implementation through the preparation of CAD, sketches and presentations (keynote / PowerPoint)
    • Recommends technical solutions to resolve complex or potential design issues
    • Able to sketch solutions to be implemented on working drawings with approval and supervision of Design Architect / Senior Design Architect
  2. Design Review and Planning Stage
    • Assist in the review of the Project Requirements and Comments
    • Contributes in the planning, implementation and incorporation of comments in the design and drawings
    • Assists the Design Architect / Senior Design Architect on the process of interpreting the drawings and comments according to the project requirement, company and design standards
  3. Design Presentations
    • Analyses the Design Review and uses it
    • Produces design presentations (Keynote / PowerPoint)
    • Produces 2D, 3D and working drawings
  4. Project Data Development (area schedule and project information)
    • Prepares and computes for the area schedule and project information of the projects and designs
  5. Project Coordination
    • Helping with the documentation of each phase of the project
  6. Team Management
    • Communicates with the team the design direction of the project
    • Delegates tasks to team members
    • Manages the team and ensures timely production/output of the project
    • Mentors and evaluates team members in realizing their professional growth
    • Maintains inter-and intra- departmental workflow by promoting a spirit of cooperation
    • Contributes to team effort by sharing call duty and working time as needed
  7. Quality Assurance
    • Assists in the review of the team’s deliverables for accuracy, efficiency and thoroughness
  8. Communication
    • Establishes and maintains professional and effective lines of communication with the superiors and other departments
    • Communicates and listens in an appropriate and professional manne

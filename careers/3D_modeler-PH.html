Job Function:

This person is responsible to create and deliver virtual output from sketches and concept art. Responsible to interpret storyboards and designs to create character and object animations.


Job Qualifications:
  • Candidate must possess at least Bachelor's/College Degree in Architecture or equivalent.
  • At least 5 Year(s) of working experience as 3D Modeller.
  • Highly Creative in Concepts, Solution Providing and Client Support.
  • Must be proactive in nature with the ability to work with less supervision.
  • Must be able to present concepts, ideas and sample internally as in client settings.
  • Experience in designing commercial buildings & hospitality projects

Job Responsibilities:

Major duties and responsibilities:
  1. Preparation of Drawings and Presentations:
    • Create and manage digital assets
    • Interpret concept art and sketches to create virtual characters, environments and props
    • Create textures and UV maps
    • Rig models and characters for animation
    • Create realistic and consistent animation
  2. Design Solutions:
    • Optimize, refine and correct model geometry
    • Resolve minor design issues
  3. Quality Assurance:
    • Prototype and iterate models and characters based on feedback from team members
    • Follow design guidelines and other technical constraints
    • Ensures that the output are in compliance to the company and design standards
  4. Communication:
    • Collaborate with design team to understand and integrate motion into models
    • Work with Design Manager and Team Leads to conceptualize and visualize character animations and effects
    • Work with the team to execute projects on timely manner
    • Communicates and listens in an appropriate and professional manner

Technical Skills:

  • Photoshop

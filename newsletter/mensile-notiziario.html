Sunshine Empire Designed by MDL is Hanoi, Vietnam’s Largest Total Investment Project

Sunshine Empire of Sunshine Group was awarded with the “Policy of Investment Decision: Project with the Largest Total Investment Amount” at the Hanoi 2020 Investment Cooperation and Development Conference held on June 27. This mixed-use commercial complex which has a total investment of more than seven trillion dong has also won a Golden Prize during the National Urban Planning, organized by the Vietnam Urban Planning and Development Association (VUPDA) in collaboration with Ministry of Construction, Ministry of Mineral and Environment, and Vietnam Chamber of Commerce & Industry last April 25, 2019. Mercurio Design Lab was commissioned by Sunshine Group as the designer for this large development project. Read more on the details of the conference at viettimes.vn.

China Daily Publishes Mercurio’s Architectural Resolution to Covid-19

Massimo Mercurio, Managing and Creative Director of Mercurio Design Lab, talks to China Daily about an architectural resolution to the environment’s recent dramatic changes caused by today’s pandemic, Covid-19. Mercurio’s ideas on designing buildings with balconies to give an impression of a greater space and smart system was printed in the June 19, 2020 issue of the newspaper along with the other interviews of known designers.

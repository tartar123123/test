Collection, Use and/or Disclosure of Cookies

Like any other website, Mercurio Design Lab uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

Cookies are tiny text files, which may include an anonymous unique identifier that allows the server to store information about the user which allow us to remember you. The cookies placed by our server are readable only by us and cookies cannot access, read or modified by any other data on an electronic device.

Should you wish to disable the cookies associated with these technologies, you may do so by changing the setting in your browser. All web-browsers offer option to refuse any cookie, and if you refuse our cookie then we do not gather any information on that visitor. However, you may not be able to enter or view certain part(s) of our website.

For more information generally on cookies, including how to disable them, please refer to aboutcookies.org. You will also find details on how to delete cookies from your computer.

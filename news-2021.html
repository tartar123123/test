Launch of Indonesia-1, Tallest Twin Tower of Indonesia

Indonesia-1 Tower was officially launched in a press conference at Bromo Ballroom, Grand Hyatt Jakarta on 27th August 2019, with speakers Tay Swee Eng, Project Director of CS Land, James Allan, President of JLL Indonesia, Arief Rahardjo, Director of Strategic Consulting of Cushman & Wakefield and Massimo Mercurio, Managing Director of Mercurio Design Lab.

Built in the center of Jakarta's Central Business District, precisely on Jalan MH Thamrin, and with a direct connection to the Bundaran HI MRT Station, this 303 meter high mixed-use development will be the tallest twin tower in Indonesia after its completion in 2021. The structure has two cubes that crown the tops of each of the towers. The northern cube is wrapped in a mesh screen and includes the residential component of the complex. The southern cube is a lightweight steel-and-glass structure and features the CS logo as a sculptural element symbolizing infinity, hinting the longevity of the company. It offers a skyline view, a restaurant, and private club.

Set to become the landmark for Jakarta, Mercurio Design Lab, the design architect of Indonesia 1, designed this skyscraper commercial development with ambitions to go beyond the purely functional.Read more »

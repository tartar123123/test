Sunshine Bay Wins Platinum Trophy in 2024 MUSE Design Awards

Mercurio Design Lab’s latest project, Sunshine Bay, recently won a Platinum trophy in the Architectural Design – Conceptual category of the 2024 MUSE Design Awards’ first season. This victory marks MDL’s return to the prestigious MUSE Awards, where it last won a Silver accolade in 2022 for iLiv@Grange.

Sunshine Bay stands as MDL’s inaugural project in Dubai’s vibrant architectural scene, with the 13-storey residential complex already making waves thanks to its unique, sailboat-inspired form. Its balconies are arrayed in gentle curves, creating a façade akin to a sail, which zigs and zags with the seaborne winds. This curvilinear tower section sits atop a podium, which in turn resembles the dual skids of a catamaran, complete with a deck-like canopy with wooden flooring.

MUSE’s Platinum award is behind only in stature to the coveted Design of the Year award, which MDL won back in 2021 with Dusit Thani Laguna. Launch of Indonesia-1, Tallest Twin Tower of Indonesia

Indonesia-1 Tower was officially launched in a press conference at Bromo Ballroom, Grand Hyatt Jakarta on 27th August 2019, with speakers Tay Swee Eng, Project Director of CS Land, James Allan, President of JLL Indonesia, Arief Rahardjo, Director of Strategic Consulting of Cushman & Wakefield and Massimo Mercurio, Managing Director of Mercurio Design Lab.

Built in the center of Jakarta's Central Business District, precisely on Jalan MH Thamrin, and with a direct connection to the Bundaran HI MRT Station, this 303 meter high mixed-use development will be the tallest twin tower in Indonesia after its completion in 2021. The structure has two cubes that crown the tops of each of the towers. The northern cube is wrapped in a mesh screen and includes the residential component of the complex. The southern cube is a lightweight steel-and-glass structure and features the CS logo as a sculptural element symbolizing infinity, hinting the longevity of the company. It offers a skyline view, a restaurant, and private club.

Set to become the landmark for Jakarta, Mercurio Design Lab, the design architect of Indonesia 1, designed this skyscraper commercial development with ambitions to go beyond the purely functional.Read more »

Among the 2,000 competing projects from 42 countries, Villa Mistral of Mercurio Design Lab has won the Platinum award for the 2019 Architectural Design, Residential category.

The Muse Creative and Muse Design Awards are international competitions for creative and design professionals from all discipline who help push the evolution of their industry in a positive direction. The awards are administered and judged by the International Awards Associates (IAA) and stellar panels of internationally-recognized professionals from the creative and design fields. All categories are ranked according to industry-best standards. During blind judging, panelists evaluate the effectiveness of creative and design concepts to select the winners. Read more »

Italy’s 2019 Best Master Planning and Interior Design Studio is Awarded to MDL

Mercurio Design Lab was awarded by LUXlife Designer Awards as Italy’s Best Master Planning & Interior Design Studio.The LUXlife Designer Awards have been designed to recognise companies, teams and individuals who are excelling in the design’s ever-growing industry. MDL Wins Multiple Design Awards for 2018

Mercurio Design Lab, an award-winning Italian design studio, received six prestigious recognitions in 2018. Silver A’ Design Awards were given to Amber Skye for the Architecture, Building and Structure Design Category - Medium Rise Residential; as well as the Cultural Exchange Center, for the Architecture, Building and Structure Design Category - Convention Centre. A Golden A’ Design Award, on the other hand, was handed to Mercurio Design Lab for Superstring at Hamilton Scotts. Amber Skye bragged another award as Winner for Residential High-Rise Architecture in Singapore at the prestigious International Property Awards for Asia Pacific. Villa Vento, meanwhile, was shortlisted for design et al’s International Architecture and Awards 2018 competition. Finally, our creative director Massimo Mercurio moved to #31 in DAC’s World’s Leading Designers ranking from last year’s #46.

MDL Opened New Offices in Asia

Mercurio Design Lab expands its operation. The year 2018 saw the opening of a thirty-strong Manila office, A.M. Associates (Philippines). In addition, new offices were registered in Shanghai, China, as well as Hanoi, Vietnam all within the same year.
